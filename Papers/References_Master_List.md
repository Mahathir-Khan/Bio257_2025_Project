# Master Reference List

1. [Insulin and serine metabolism as sex-specific hallmarks of Alzheimer's disease in the human hippocampus](https://doi.org/10.1016/j.celrep.2022.111271)  
   *Bulk RNA-seq dataset (NCBI BioProject 739253) exploring hippocampal insulin and serine metabolism as sex-specific features of Alzheimer’s disease.*

2. [Gut microbiome alterations in Alzheimer’s disease](https://doi.org/10.1038/s41598-017-13601-y)  
   *16S rRNA dataset (ENA PRJEB51982) linking microbial composition shifts to cognitive decline and amyloidosis in AD.*

3. [A single-cell and spatial RNA-seq database for Alzheimer’s disease (ssREAD)](https://doi.org/10.1038/s41467-024-49133-z)  
   *Public database integrating single-cell and spatial transcriptomics across AD brain regions for pathway-level analyses.*

4. [GENCODE: Human gene annotation (GRCh38)](https://www.gencodegenes.org/human/)  
   *Reference transcriptome used for RNA-seq read alignment and gene quantification.*

5. [Implications of gut and oral microbiota in neuroinflammatory responses in Alzheimer’s disease](https://doi.org/10.1016/j.lfs.2023.122132)  
   *Reviews microbiome–neuroinflammation links and discusses oral–gut microbial contributions to AD progression.*

6. [Genetic correlations between Alzheimer’s disease and gut microbiome genera](https://doi.org/10.1038/s41598-023-31730-5)  
   *Genome-wide analysis identifying overlapping genetic architecture between AD and microbiome composition.*

7. [Amyloid-β and phosphorylated tau accumulations cause abnormalities at synapses of Alzheimer’s disease neurons](https://doi.org/10.3233/JAD-160612)  
    *Demonstrates synaptic structural and biochemical disruptions caused by hallmark AD proteins.*

8. [Neuroinflammatory signaling in the pathogenesis of Alzheimer's disease](https://doi.org/10.2174/1570159X19666210826130210)  
    *Comprehensive review of molecular inflammatory cascades contributing to AD pathology.*

9. [The role of NLRP3 inflammasome in Alzheimer's disease and potential therapeutic targets](https://doi.org/10.3389/fphar.2022.845185)  
    *Highlights inflammasome activation as a therapeutic focus for AD-associated neuroinflammation.*

10. [Activation of microglia and astrocytes: a roadway to neuroinflammation and Alzheimer’s disease](https://doi.org/10.1007/s10787-019-00580-x)  
    *Summarizes glial activation mechanisms and downstream inflammatory signaling in neurodegeneration.*

11. [Antibiotic-induced perturbations in gut microbial diversity influence neuro-inflammation and amyloidosis in a murine model of Alzheimer’s disease](https://www.nature.com/articles/srep1505)  
    *Experimental evidence connecting microbiome depletion to amyloid pathology.*

12. [Interactions between the microbiota, immune, and nervous systems in health and disease](https://doi.org/10.1038/nn.4476)  
    *Foundational overview of gut–brain–immune crosstalk and implications for neurological disease.*

13. [The microbiome: composition and locations](https://doi.org/10.1016/bs.pmbts.2020.08.013)  
    *Outlines microbial distribution across human body sites and methodological advances in profiling.*

14. [Gut microbiota distinguishes aging Hispanics with Alzheimer’s disease: associations with cognitive impairment and severity](https://doi.org/10.1038/s41598-025-13262-2)  
    *Recent multi-ethnic study identifying microbiome signatures correlated with cognitive decline in aging populations.*

15. [Single-cell RNA sequencing analysis of human Alzheimer’s disease brain samples reveals neuronal and glial-specific differential expression](https://doi.org/10.1371/journal.pone.0277630)  
    *Single-cell profiling uncovering cell-type–specific transcriptomic changes in AD cortex.*

16. [Probability-one homotopy methods for constrained clustering](https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/pii/S037704271830XXXX)  
    *Optimization framework ensuring global convergence for constrained clustering — applicable to omics data integration.*

17. [Darmanis et al., Human Single-cell RNA-seq (GSE67835)](https://www.pnas.org/doi/full/10.1073/pnas.1507125112)  
    *Single-cell RNA-seq reference for human brain tissue; dataset deposited in GEO (GSE67835).*

18. [European Nucleotide Archive (ENA) – Project PRJEB86129](https://www.ebi.ac.uk/ena/browser/view/PRJEB86129)  
    *Repository entry providing raw sequence reads used for microbiome comparative analyses.*

19. [National Institute on Aging – Alzheimer’s Disease Fact Sheet](https://www.nia.nih.gov/health/alzheimers-and-dementia/alzheimers-disease-fact-sheet)  
    *Summary of clinical and biological aspects of AD for contextual reference.*
